Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026 · Non Son Analytics

Non Son Analytics ("the Service") is an internal analytics dashboard operated by Non Son, a hat and helmet retail chain in Vietnam. The Service helps Non Son monitor the performance of TikTok accounts operated by its own retail stores.

1. Information We Collect

When a store staff member connects a TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit, we collect, with the account holder's explicit authorization, the following data provided by the TikTok API: basic profile information (display name, avatar, open ID), account statistics (follower count, likes count, video count), and metadata and public statistics of the account's videos (title, posting time, view/like/comment/share counts).

We do NOT collect passwords, private messages, draft content, or any data beyond the scopes explicitly authorized (user.info.basic, user.info.profile, user.info.stats, video.list).

2. How We Use the Information

Data is used solely for internal business analytics: measuring content performance, channel growth, and posting consistency across Non Son's own store accounts. Data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored in a secured Supabase (PostgreSQL) database. Access tokens are encrypted at rest. Access to the dashboard is limited to authorized Non Son management staff.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Snapshot data is retained for analytics purposes and periodically aggregated. An account holder may revoke access at any time from the TikTok app (Settings → Security → Apps and websites) or by contacting us; upon revocation or request, the account's tokens are deleted and data collection stops immediately.
